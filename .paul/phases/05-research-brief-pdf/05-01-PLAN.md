## Goal
Author the v1.1 research brief as a review-ready markdown source with embedded statistics, chart figures, and a methodology-first narrative. Deliverables: a stats JSON, three PNG charts, and a polished markdown brief — everything needed by Plan 05-02 to render the final institutional PDF.

## Purpose
The funnel IS the product. This plan turns v1.0's 141 mature strong_buy signals and +9.0% alpha into a written, auditable narrative that explains *how* the filter works and *why* the numbers are defensible. A hedge fund analyst reading the brief should be able to describe our methodology to their PM without us in the room.

## Output
- `backend/exports/brief_stats.py` — pure computation: queries Neo4j + reads Phase 4 CSV, emits `stats.json`
- `backend/exports/brief_charts.py` — reads `stats.json` + Phase 4 CSV, writes three PNG charts

- `.paul/phases/05-research-brief-pdf/brief_v1_1.md` — the full brief, ready for Plan 05-02 to render

       - By num_insiders bucket: {"2": stats, "3": stats, "4": stats, "5+": stats}; each with n, hit_rate, mean_alpha

      - "Why filing-date returns" — avoids look-ahead bias (transaction date is private; filing is the earliest public moment).
