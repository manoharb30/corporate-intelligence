## Backend APIs available
- GET /api/snapshot/weekly?days=N — signal list (strong_buy clusters with returns)
- GET /api/event-detail/{accession} — signal detail (buyers, Form 4 URLs)
- GET /api/signal-performance — all signal performances
- GET /api/signal-performance/summary — aggregate stats
- GET /api/signal-performance/download — CSV
- GET /api/explorer/expand/person/{name} — cross-company connections
