**Status:** Complete

## Result

Hypothesis partially validated. 3 groups tested on 85 matured signals:

| Group | n | Hit Rate | Mean Alpha |
|---|---|---|---|
| No activist | 59 | 69.5% | +6.2% |
| Activist BEFORE insiders | 16 | 62.5% | +27.2% |
| Activist AFTER insiders | 10 | 80.0% | +13.8% |

- Activist BEFORE = highest alpha but lowest hit rate (binary)
- Activist AFTER = highest hit rate (insiders were early, activist confirms)

## Decision

Not actionable as a filter. Temporal sequencing matters but sample too small (16/10) for a production rule. Insight noted for Neudata narrative.
