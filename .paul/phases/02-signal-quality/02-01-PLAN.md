<objective>
## Goal
Implement 3 evidence-based exclusion rules that push strong_buy hit rate from 62% to 70%+ while retaining 100+ signals (80 minimum).

## Purpose
Hedge fund clients (Citadel, Squarepoint, Bridgewater, Neudata) need a high-quality signal. 62% hit rate is borderline — 70%+ is the threshold for institutional credibility. Research identified 3 statistically significant failure patterns; this plan turns them into a production filter.

```gherkin

And a filter_summary is added to classified.json output
```

    1. Queries Neo4j for ALL strong_buy signals (2+ buyers, $100K+, midcap, GENUINE, Dec 2024 - Apr 2026)

       - earnings_distance: days to next earnings via yfinance (cache results)
