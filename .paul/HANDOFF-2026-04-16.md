### Signal Quality (Phase 2)
- **Plan 02-01:** Built earnings proximity filter with TDD (12 tests)
  - Rule: exclude signals where next earnings >60 days away
  - Research-backed: p=0.003 statistical significance
  - Rationale: mid-quarter buys = informational asymmetry window
- **Plan 02-02:** Data integrity verification — 18/18 signals verified at 0.000% discrepancy against live yfinance
- **Plan 02-03:** Retroactive filter applied to 263 days

### Research Findings (from 3 parallel agents)
1. **Earnings cycle** is dominant predictor (p=0.003) — mid-quarter buys outperform
2. **Momentum is U-shaped** — near-highs and deeply-crashed both outperform "purgatory" middle
3. **Volatility, sector ETF momentum, prior selling** — NOT significant
4. **Sector identity** matters (Basic Materials/Comms worst) but sample too small for hard rule
5. **Cluster value inversely correlated** — mega ($5M+) clusters underperform
